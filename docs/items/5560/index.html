What are the benefits of Open Access?

  1. Teachers, practicioners and anyone else without access to research through an institution can access and use research to improve their work
  2. Academics in developing countries can access research and stay up to date in their fields
  3. Public Access: Taxpayers feel they get value for their money
  4. Increased visibility of research
  5. More citations (due to more visibility)
  6. Compliant with funders (and the Research Excellence Framework)
  7. More transparency so academics can replicate and validate previous research
  8. When data is shared openly it can be used in other studies; reducing time and effort
  9. More accurate reporting from journalists to the public
  10. Open Access publications can help inform policy
